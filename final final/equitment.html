Welcome to All About Golf

This page goes over some of the main golf gear you’ll need. We’ll cover clubs like drivers and putters, plus extra stuff like gloves and tees. It’ll help you know what each item does and why it matters.

Driver – Used for big tee shots. It has a large head and is good for getting max distance.

Hybrids – They mix woods and irons. Good for harder shots and easier to hit for new players.

Irons – Best for shots to the green. Lower number = longer shot. Higher = more loft, less distance.

Putters – You’ll use this on the green to finish off. Different shapes depending what you like.

Golf Accessories:

  • Tees – Tiny stands for the ball when teeing off.
  • Ball Markers – Used to mark where your ball was on the green.
  • Gloves – Help you grip better and stop blisters.
  • Towels – Good to keep your hands and clubs clean mid game.
