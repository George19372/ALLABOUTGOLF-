Golf Etiquette

  • Being Quiet

    Stay silent while someone else is swinging.

  • Pace of Play

    Keep the game moving — 18 holes should take 3.5 to 4 hrs approx.

  • Bunkers

    Always rake it after you’ve hit out.

  • Divots

    Put the grass you tore out back if you can.

  • Pitch Marks

    Fix any pitch marks you make on the greens.

  • Letting Faster Groups Play Through

    If your group is slow, let them go past.

  • Safety

    Always yell “Fore!” if the ball might hit someone nearby.

  • Phones & Music

    Phones should be silent and keep music quiet too.
